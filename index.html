COMPANY PROFILE

MV Lien Minh Ltd Co., was established in 2003. Since then, we're an exclusive agency of Leonhard Kurz Stiftung & Co. KG in Vietnam market, which is the leading company in hot stamping foil manufacturing and its related technologies.

Hot stamping decoration and finishes can be found on a almost everything surrounding us, from books, greeting cards, wallets, shoes, automobiles, tickets, panels on electronics devices to furniture. The application possibilities are virtually endless.

Apart from its versatility, it is also a quick and dry, environmentally friendly process.

As a part of the worldwide KURZ network of more than 9 manufacturing plants, 15 subsidiaries and over 80 exclusive agencies, we have our head office in Ho Chi Minh City. Today, we serve over 500 companies of all sizes and profiles in various industries in Vietnam alone.
